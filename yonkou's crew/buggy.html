Cross Guild

Cross Guild is an organization that can use its battle power and political influence to put bounties on Marines in a reversal of how pirates get bounties from the World Government.
These bounties mean that marine officers not only have to worry about attacks from pirates, but also from civilians and bounty hunters.
This means even the most beloved Marines are vulnerable to being stabbed in the back by the very civilians they protect; although in the newspapers the real cause of the death of the Marine is omitted.
