MUGIWARAS

The Mugiwaras are a pirate crew, which has as their captain are the Yonkou Monkey D. Luffy.
They are knowns for their adventures at the grandline, and they are search for the legendary treasure, The One Piece.

MEMBERS: Luffy; Zoro; Nami; Usopp; Sanji; Chopper; Robin; Franky; Brook; Jinbe
